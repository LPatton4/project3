About Me

My name is Leah Patton. I am a first year student at IIT and I am majoring in Digital Humanities. I am still not too sure what I want to do with my degree after I graduate, but I have enjoyed the courses that I have taken so far.

I was born and raised on the South Side of Chicago. I have never traveled outside of the United States, but I have lots of dream destinations that I would like to visit one one day. I enjoy learning about other cultures, so I do so from home by studying the maps of other countries, trying to learn foreign languages, and watching travel related content online.

I have always been a relatively active person. When I was very young, I took gymnastics classes. By the time that I was 4 years old, I started taking ballet classes and continued until I was 18 years old. Because of the pandemic, classes have now become less accessible, so I still dance from home, but not nearly as often. I also participated in band classes from the time that I was 7 years old until I graduated high school.
